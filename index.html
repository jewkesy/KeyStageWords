"The Parent Bit..."

This application was created to help my daughter practice the Key Stage and High Frequency words taught at primary school.

The words range from Reception Year 1 to Year 6, displayed using flashcards in decks of . There are currently 3500 words.

A few design considerations have been implemented: -
Use a flashcard approach
Rather than specify the difficulty of the words, which can be daunting to a child, I've used different colours
Minimal on-screen content to prevent distractions
Lightweight, scaleable design, suitable for a range of devices
The last flashcard is a cute, fluffy kitten as a reward!

The application is free to use, to reference and to share. Any queries, let me know!
